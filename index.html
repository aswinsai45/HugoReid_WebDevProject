Who Are We

Hugo Reid Elementary School in Arcadia, California, was founded in 1958. It has since been providing education and serving the community for several decades. Hugo Reid Elementary School is a distinguished educational institution situated in the heart of California, USA. Nestled within our vibrant community, our esteemed elementary school boasts a storied history of delivering exceptional education to our young learners. academically and personally. With a team of dedicated educators, state-of-the-art facilities, and an unyielding focus on instilling a deep love for learning, Hugo Reid Elementary School proudly stands as an educational cornerstone in our California community, diligently preparing our students for a bright and prosperous future.

Academics at HRES

At Hugo Reid, we take pride in fostering a dynamic and enriching learning environment for our students. We believe that education goes beyond textbooks, and our students are encouraged to explore, question, and innovate. At Hugo Reid, assessment and evaluation are not just about measuring progress; they are essential tools for continuous improvement. We celebrate achievements, both big and small, and take pride in the accomplishments of our students in various academic endeavors!
